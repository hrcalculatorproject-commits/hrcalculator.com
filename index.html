Key features
  • Export your builds as an easy-to-read image, or export selected loadouts together as one big scrollable image
  • Detailed build planner for Helmet, Chest, Boots, Weapon, and Ring with Raw, Super, and Mega Powerstones in every socket
  • Combined Build Stats, Overall Combined Stats, Power Stone Gearscore, Titan Gear Score, and Overall Gear Score
  • Titan Gear planner with levels, enchants, enchant ranks, effective rank calculation, Titan enchant totals, and a set-all Titan level control
  • Per-build pet selection with grouped pet families, aura effects, and ability reference
  • Reused item summaries that show which loadouts share the same Titan item and Powerstones
  • Unlimited saved loadouts with local storage, plus save, load, delete, import/export raw build data, and export summary screenshot controls
